Frequently Asked Questions

NLEX Music is a music project created by Noli Rodenas, a music producer from the Philippines, specializing in electronic genres like Progressive House, Melodic Bass, and Future Pop.

NLEX graduated from Musixmatch Academy in December 2023-2024. He also studied basic music theory at Music Gateway School. His passion for songwriting began in 2019 on his mobile phone, and through dedication and experimentation, he has developed the skills to create his own original tracks.

Creating your own music can be challenging, especially for beginners. It requires a blend of creativity, technical skills, and patience.

NLEX is currently a second-year Information Technology student at Pinamalayan Maritime Foundation Technological College Incorporated (PMFTCI) while continuing to pursue his passion for music production.

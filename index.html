Frequently Asked Questions

NLEX Music is a music project created by Noli Rodenas, a music producer from the Philippines, specializing in electronic genres like Progressive House, Melodic Bass, and Future Pop.

NLEX is graduated on Musixmatch Academy in class of December 2023-2024. He has also studied basic music theory at Music Gateway School. His passion for songwriting began in 2019 on his mobile phone, and through dedication and experimentation, he has developed the skills to create his own original tracks.

Creating your own music can be challenging, especially for beginners. It requires a blend of creativity, technical skills, and patienc.
